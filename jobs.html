Cybersecurity Specialist

Reference Number: CS789

Company: SecureTech Solutions

Location: Sydney, Australia

Salary Range: $90,000 - $120,000 per year

Reports To: Head of Information Security

Job Overview

We are looking for a skilled Cybersecurity Specialist to safeguard our IT infrastructure, data, and networks from cyber threats.

Key Responsibilities

  • Monitor network security and prevent unauthorized access.
  • Conduct vulnerability assessments and risk analysis.
  • Develop security policies and protocols.
  • Investigate security breaches and implement corrective measures.
  • Collaborate with IT teams to strengthen overall security.

Required Qualifications & Skills

Essential:
  • Bachelor's degree in Cybersecurity, Computer Science, or related field.
  • 3+ years of experience in information security.
  • Knowledge of firewalls, encryption, and security frameworks.
  • Experience with penetration testing and threat analysis.
  • Certifications such as CISSP or CEH.
Preferred:
  • Master's degree in Cybersecurity.
  • Experience in cloud security (AWS, Azure, GCP).
  • Programming skills in Python or C++.
