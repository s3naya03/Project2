Data Analyst

Reference Number: DA456

Company: DataInsight Solutions

Location: Melbourne, Australia

Salary Range: $85,000 - $110,000 per year

Reports To: Head of Analytics

Job Overview

We are seeking a skilled Data Analyst to transform complex data into actionable insights that drive business decisions. The ideal candidate will have strong analytical skills and experience with data visualization tools.

Key Responsibilities

  • Collect, clean, and interpret large datasets
  • Create reports and dashboards to visualize data
  • Identify trends and patterns in complex data sets
  • Collaborate with teams to implement data-driven solutions
  • Maintain databases and data systems

Required Qualifications & Skills

Essential:
  • Bachelor's degree in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science or related field
  • 2+ years experience in data analysis or related field
  • Proficiency in SQL and Excel
  • Experience with data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI)
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
Preferred:
  • Experience with Python or R for data analysis
  • Knowledge of machine learning concepts
  • Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure)
